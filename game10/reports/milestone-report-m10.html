Milestone Deep Dives
Feature Spotlight
M6 — Skill Trees
Three-Path Mastery System

A branching skill tree with 36 nodes across Warrior, Ranger, and Mage paths. Passive nodes auto-apply on unlock via the character sheet, while active skills are dispatched directly from the hotbar with animated cooldown overlays.

36 Nodes 3 Paths Hotbar Dispatch Z/X/C/V Keys Cooldown Pies Auto-Passives
M8 — Spells & Magic
Full Spellcasting Engine

Ten distinct spell types with unique cast mechanics, particle systems, and status effect interactions. The Elder Stone Golem — an 800 HP dual-phase boss — anchors the combat challenge, complete with a ground-slam AoE and a dedicated top-screen HP bar.

10 Spells 6 Status Effects Mana Economy Boss Fight 800 HP Boss 2-Phase AI
M7 — Mining & Crafting
Resource Economy

Channel-based ore mining with 1.5s progress bars, depleting nodes that respawn after 5 minutes, and 20 recipes across Forge and Enchant tabs.

6 Ore Types 20 Recipes Forge + Enchant
M9 — World & Quests
Quest & World Layer

Eight quests spanning kill, gear, material, boss, and legendary objectives. World map overlay shows Zone 1 and locked Zone 2. Elite enemy variants carry guaranteed Rare+ drops.

8 Quests World Map Elite Enemies
M10 — Polish & Save
Player Experience Layer

Versioned localStorage save/load, auto-save every 60s, main menu with class selection, floating-origin touch joystick, and full gamepad support with rumble on hit.

Save System Gamepad Touch Controls

Transparency Report
Known Issues & Gaps
#001
Zone 2 Transition Portal Not Fully Wired
Zone 2 data exists and is referenced in the world map overlay, but the portal trigger that moves the player between zones does not fire reliably in all spawn configurations. Players may not be able to enter Zone 2 in all sessions.
OPEN M11 Target
#002
Boss Spawn Proximity Not Communicated to Player
The Elder Stone Golem spawns only when the player approaches the portal area, but no UI cue or ambient hint alerts the player to this mechanic. The boss may appear unexpectedly or never if the player avoids the area.
OPEN M11 Target
#003
Touch Controls Need Polish on Small Screens
The floating-origin joystick and attack/skill buttons function correctly but hitbox sizing is not optimised for screens below 390px wide. Some skill buttons may be difficult to activate reliably in fast combat.
PLANNED M15 Target
#004
Gamepad Rumble Not Supported on All Browsers
The Gamepad Haptics API is implemented and fires on hit, but Firefox and some mobile browsers do not support the rumble actuator. The feature degrades silently to a no-op — no error is thrown and gameplay continues normally.
GRACEFUL NO-OP Browser Limitation
#005
No Audio System Yet (Stubs Only)
Audio function stubs are in place across the codebase — spell casts, hit impacts, and ambient music hooks are all called — but no audio assets exist and the Web Audio context is never initialised. The game is completely silent.
PLANNED M11-M12 Target
#006
Spell Visual Effect Quality Variance
Fireball has a high-quality multi-layer particle system. Several other spells (notably Rain of Arrows and Whirlwind) use simpler placeholder visuals that do not match the visual bar set by the fire spells. No gameplay impact.
COSMETIC M12 Polish Pass
#007
Save/Load Equipment Re-hydration Edge Cases
Core character stats, inventory contents, and quest progress save and load correctly. However, equipment items with procedurally-generated affixes can occasionally fail to re-attach their affix data on load, resulting in a "clean" version of the item.
OPEN M11 Fix
