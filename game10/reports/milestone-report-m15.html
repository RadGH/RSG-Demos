M12 — Combat Polish

Game Juice

Every interaction now has weight, feedback, and visual clarity. Before → after deltas below.

⚔
Hit-Stop

Before

Hits registered silently. Animation continued uninterrupted. Combat felt weightless.

After

3 frozen frames on heavy hits, 4 on player damage. Blows land with physical conviction.
⍑
Screen Shake

Before

Static viewport. Player death, boss slams, and explosions produced no spatial feedback.

After

Camera.shake() triggers on player hit, boss slam, and death. Intensity decays exponentially.
☀
Sprite Flash

Before

Damage application invisible unless HP bar moved. No visual confirmation of contact.

After

White flash overlay for 0.15s on any hit. Instant readable feedback at a glance.
⇓
Death Animation

Before

Enemies disappeared instantly on HP reaching zero. Kills felt abrupt and unsatisfying.

After

Enemies sink downward and fade to transparent over 1 full second. Defeat reads clearly.
◆
Loot Pop

Before

Item drops appeared as static icons on the ground with no announcement.

After

Rarity-colored item name floats upward from drop point. Rare loot feels like an event.
★
Combat Numbers

Before

Uniform white numbers for all damage types. Crits, heals, and misses looked identical.

After

Crits: 1.4× size, golden, bounce. Heals: green, float up. Misses: dim, small, fade fast.

HP 800 → 400  |  67% – 33%

All projectile restrictions removed. Boss fires in every direction simultaneously. Safe windows shrink to single-frame gaps. Full aggression until death.
