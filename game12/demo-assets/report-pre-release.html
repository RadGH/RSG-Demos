⚡
Core Mechanics
🪙
10 Pennies
Pennies drop into the void one at a time. First finger to tap one claims it. +1 point. Each penny has an 8-second lifetime — unclaimed pennies vanish.
🐛
Bug Shooting
Bugs fly at each player from off-screen. Tap to shoot: +2 pts. Let one reach you: −1 pt. Bugs escalate across 6 wave tiers during a round.
✋
Multitouch
Both players touch simultaneously — full multitouch. Up to 5 touches per frame, each resolving independently against bugs (priority) then pennies.
🏆
Scoring
When all 10 pennies are resolved (claimed or expired), the round ends. Most points wins. Scores can go negative. No floor clamping.
◼
The Void — Design Principle
The background is #000000.
Not styled black. Not themed black.
The void.
Every pixel of color belongs to a game object. Nothing else exists.

The void is load-bearing. When a copper penny appears on pure black, it has maximum visual contrast and weight. Any background element steals from that. The emptiness is not laziness — it maximizes cognitive focus on the only two things that matter: the pennies and the bugs.

Architecture
src/
state.js
Single source of truth. All systems read/write gameState. No distributed state.
src/
input.js
touchstart + mousedown. Multi-touch via changedTouches. Drain queue once per tick.
src/
renderer.js
DPR-aware canvas. Layered draw fns by priority. Clear to #000000 each frame.
src/entities/
penny.js
Drop-in animation, lifetime fade, expiry flicker, circular hit test.
src/entities/
bug.js
Direction vector to target half, sinusoidal wobble, canvas-drawn body/legs.
src/entities/
particle.js
Pre-allocated pool of 128. spawnBurst(), gravity, alpha fade. Zero allocs in hot path.
src/systems/
collision.js
Bugs resolved before pennies. Per-touch entity resolution. No ghost hits.
src/audio/
synth.js
Web Audio API only. Zero audio files. OscillatorNode + noise for all sounds.
src/fx/
flash.js
Half-screen or full-screen flash. Multiple flashes queue without cancelling.

Design Risks
HIGH
Multitouch conflicts on iOS Safari — Two players touching simultaneously may trigger system gestures (screenshot, app switcher). Need to call preventDefault() on all touch events and test on real hardware.
MED
Center seam penny balance — 20% of pennies spawn in the contested seam zone. Could create overwhelming conflict or feel unfair. May need playtesting to tune the percentage.
MED
Audio unlock on iOS — AudioContext must be created on a user gesture. If the unlock flow fails silently, the game runs with no audio and looks broken. Must handle and recover gracefully.
LOW
P2 orientation disorientation — First-time P2 players may need a moment to parse the rotated UI. The layout is self-evident after 3 seconds of play but onboarding could be rough.
