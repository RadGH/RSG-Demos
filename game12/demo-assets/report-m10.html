Known Bugs at M10

MINORWheat penny at 3pts overrides combo bonus — collecting wheat at x3+ combo is the same as collecting at x1.

MINORSeries tie-round doesn't award a win — could theoretically drag a best-of-3 indefinitely if every round ties.

MINORPenny pulse uses Date.now() directly — all pennies pulse in phase. Id-based offset would look better.
