Known Issues

Bugs & Concerns

  • ⚠️
    Event Chain — Overly Aggressive Clear Clearing pendingEncounter on every travel tick may be too aggressive — rapid travel taps can skip a follow-up encounter before it fires.
  • ⚠️
    Skill Rank-Up Visibility Gap Progression resets on death by design, but there is no indicator showing "close to ranking up X" until after the first use of that skill. Players discover their progress retroactively.
  • ⚠️
    Reputation Save Edge Case The reputation system reads visitedSettlements but this key may not persist correctly across all save/load paths — needs cross-slot testing.
  • ⚠️
    Step Indicator — No Transition Animation The character creation step indicator updates state correctly but does not animate between steps — the change feels abrupt on mobile.
  • 🐛
    Coward Flee — Message Without Effect Enemy coward flee logic displays a "they flee!" message but does not remove the enemy from the combat state — it continues to act on subsequent turns as though nothing happened.
Design Notes

Suggestions & Ideas

  • 🏃
    Enemy Flee — Real Consequence The fleeAttempt flag needs to actually end the fight or present a "let them run / give chase" choice. Right now it is purely cosmetic.
  • 🎓
    Skill Caps — Background Synergy Background-granted free skills feel underused. They should count toward the rank-up threshold so starting characters feel meaningfully ahead in their specialty.
  • 🗣️
    Rumor Payoffs — Real World Flags Rumors should occasionally reference active world flags. "That merchant" on a rumor board becomes the wandering_merchant encounter two days later.
  • 🌿
    Chain Branching — World Memory Event chains could branch based on prior world flags. If you have met a soldier before, the road_stranger dialogue opens a different path.
  • 📊
    Mid-Run Stats Screen Players have no way to view current run statistics while alive. A mid-run stats overlay — accessible from the journey screen — would surface engagement data and reinforce investment.

Wishlist — The Rune Stones

◆
I 💾
Mid-Run Save Points
Checkpoints for long sessions — rest at a safe settlement to mark your progress without triggering full permadeath.
II ⚖️
Faction Reputation
Three factions, each tracking your deeds. Reputation shifts encounter tables, prices, and which doors open.
III 🌙
Day/Night Encounters
Night travel is faster but draws from a darker pool. Predators hunt. Strange lights appear. The road is not the same after dark.
IV 🧙
Companion NPC
A character who joins temporarily — their own personality, morale threshold, and reason for walking this road.
V 🔨
Camp Crafting Table
Combine two ingredients into something useful. Medicine from herbs. Torches from wood and fat. Decisions at rest.
VI 🗺️
Map Reveal
The route uncovers itself as you travel. Fog lifts. Landmarks emerge. The road behind feels earned.
VII 🐴
Mounted Travel
A horse reduces water cost per segment but demands feed daily. Faster with upkeep — a strategic resource gamble.
VIII 🍂
Seasonal Events
Time of year reshapes the encounter pool. Winter starves the road. Harvest season fills it. The world does not stand still.
