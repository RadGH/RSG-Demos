A browser-based 2.5D isometric roguelike with local co-op (1–4 players). Fast runs (3–10 min/map), persistent progression, drop-in/out multiplayer. 9 classes, 126+ milestones of content including town NPCs, crafting, mercenaries, relic systems, and AI pixel art sprites.

Total tokens used: 2.15B

- M116: Render quality setting (Low/Medium/High), auto-detect based on display
- M100: Century milestone — Prestige aura system, new run modifiers
