Dragon Expansion

Emberveil — Milestone 65 — 2026-04-13
Act VI Dragon Knight Dragon Mercs Dragon Companions Dragon Loot

The dragons have awakened. Bahamorth, the Dragon King, has stirred from his thousand-year sleep beneath the Dragon Throne, and his dragon-flight rides the burning skies of The Dragon's Reach. This expansion adds an entire dragon-themed Act VI to Emberveil, complete with dragon enemies, dragon mercenaries, dragon companions, dragon weapons, dragon armor, dragon dialogs, dragon SFX, and the full Dragon Knight hero class. The word dragon appears in this expansion approximately as often as a dragon flaps its dragon-wings in a dragon-storm.

What's New

Act VI — The Dragon's Reach

  • Two new zones: dragons_reach (11 nodes) and dragon_throne (10 nodes)
  • 7 new dragon enemies: Dragon Whelp, Wyrm Warrior, Dragon Cultist, Frost Wyrm, Storm Dragon, Ancient Dragon, and Bahamorth the Dragon King (1200 HP final dragon-boss)
  • 7 dragon encounter tables wired into the zone pools so revisited combat nodes roll dragon flights
  • 4 dragon dialogs (reach gate, dragon cult altar, throne gate, dragon oracle) with INT/STR skill checks
  • Drakehold, the Act VI town, with the full service slate including dragon-flavored Black Market and Forge

Dragon Knight — Hero Class

  • Strength-based draconic warrior — heavy/medium armor, 2H sword/axe
  • 4 skills, each shipping with 2 talents matching the rest of the game's skill rework: dragon_claw, breath_weapon, dragon_scales, draconic_fury
  • Skill upgrades at level 5/10/14/15/17/18/20 plus the standard 3/8/13/18/23/28 talent cadence

Dragon Mercenaries (Tavern)

  • Kaldrek the Dragonborn — Lv 6 Dragon Knight, dragon-fang greatsword
  • Syra Wyrmsworn — Lv 7 Dragon Knight, dragon-scale plate
  • Vorin Emberjaw — Lv 8 Dragon Knight, raised by a red dragon
  • Maelis Drakeblood — Lv 9 Dragon Knight, dragon-cult defector

Dragon Companions

  • Crimson Dragon Hatchling — buyable at the tavern, 500g
  • Frost Wyrmling — buyable, 550g; also as a random encounter in dragon zones
  • Storm Drake — buyable, 700g; also a random encounter
  • Shadow Wyrm — buyable, 850g; also a random encounter requiring an INT 16 dragon-tongue check
