Major Features

⚔

100% Skill Implementation

All 84 skills across 20 hero classes are now fully wired and functional. Every active, passive, and talent ability triggers correctly in combat with proper damage calculations, status effects, and visual feedback.

Core System
⚖

Combat Rebalance

A 50% damage scaling nerf brings fights into a satisfying rhythm instead of one-shot territory. Monte Carlo balance testing across thousands of simulated encounters validates the new curves. New affixes -- life steal, mana steal, and magic resist -- add meaningful itemization depth.

Rework
🐶

10 Class Pet Companions

Talent-unlocked companions join your party in combat. Each has unique pixel art portraits, idle and attack animations, and distinct combat behaviors. From the loyal Bone Hound to the ethereal Spirit Wisp, companions add tactical variety to every class.

New
🎨

Character Portraits Everywhere

Hero portraits now appear in the tavern hire screen, party overview, inventory panels, skill trees, and combat HUD. Every screen where you interact with a character shows their face.

Polish
🎵

60+ Sound Effects & Music

ElevenLabs-generated audio brings the world to life. Combat hits, spell casts, level-up fanfares, menu clicks, and 3 original music tracks (title, combat, exploration) replace all placeholder beeps.

New
🖼

Sprite Replacement Tool

A new tool page in the asset gallery lets you manage and swap character graphics without touching code. Upload new pixel art, preview it in context, and hot-swap sprites across the entire game.

Development Timeline

M46-M53
Foundation & Core Systems
Party-based RPG framework, 5-act world map, town services, basic combat loop, initial 20 hero classes with placeholder art.
M54-M58
Skill System & Portraits
84 skills wired across all classes. SpriteCook pixel art portraits generated for every hero, enemy, and boss. Character portraits integrated into all UI screens.
M59-M62
Combat Overhaul
50% damage scaling nerf, Monte Carlo balance testing, new affixes (life steal, mana steal, magic resist), forge system, black market vendor.
M63-M64
Companions & Audio
10 companion creatures with portraits and animations. 60+ ElevenLabs SFX, 3 music tracks. Map backgrounds, menu parallax, achievements system.
M65+
Tap Weapons & Multi-Town
Interactive tap combat system, multiple towns with scaling merchants, debug mode, and continued polish.
