The story

Early-game difficulty quietly fell apart. Tap weapons stacked with spell power, and a single Cleric could mass-resurrect a dead party at 100% HP — the fight was over before the revived hero could even be targeted again. Every encounter trended toward zero-risk.

Underneath, the math was worse than the symptoms. Only one affix of fifteen was actually wired into combat (of_mana_regen at CombatScreen.js:1295). Gold find at 2000% produced ~80 gold. Hit chance affixes rolled on gear but never summed. The numbers on every item were theater.

And there was no source of truth. Formulas drifted between the design docs, the combat screen, and the player's head. You could not answer "why did I take 12?" without reading three files.

Part 1 of the overhaul stops the bleeding. Extract the math. Lock it with tests. Audit what's orphaned. Fix the three biggest difficulty offenders. Ship the tools that let Part 2 rebalance from data instead of vibes.

Pipeline

The new damage pipeline

Every stage a hit passes through, quoted from src/game/formulas.js.

1 · Raw damage

Roll the attacker's base damage window.

[max(1, round(STR*0.8 + equipBonus)), max(3, round(STR*2 + equipBonus*1.5))]
▼

2 · Hit roll

Does it connect at all?

clamp(actor.hit − target.dodge, 5, 95)
▼

3 · Dodge

Derived rating, capped by role.

companion ? min(15, 3 + round(DEX*0.35)) : min(40, 5 + round(DEX*0.8))
▼

4 · Block (shields only)

Roll Block Chance. On success, subtract Block Power.

if rand() < blockChance: dmg −= blockPower
▼

5 · Armor / Resist (by type)

Physical → armor. Magic → resist. True → passes through.

/* current */ max(ceil(rawDmg * 0.15), rawDmg − armor) /* new M83 */ max(0, rawDmg − max(0, armor − pen))
▼

6 · HP

Final damage applied. Logs "deflected" (phys) or "resisted" (magic) on full mitigation.

hp = max(0, hp − final)

Cleric Resurrect rework

Before

  • Mass single-cast revive — everyone dead comes back
  • 100% HP on revive
  • No cooldown within a fight
  • Revived ally could be picked off before their turn
  • Effectively removed the fail state

After

  • Single target only
  • 25% HP on revive (scrolls & potions bumped to match)
  • 12-round cooldown, per-combat (resets each fight)
  • Damage immunity until the revived hero's next action
  • Talent: bump heal%; Talent: extend immunity +1 round

Armor math — three models

Damage taken from a 50-damage hit as armor scales 0→100. Old model leaves chip damage even at huge armor. New flat model hits zero cleanly. The speculative % curve is plotted only for comparison — it is not shipping.
