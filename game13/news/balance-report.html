Overview

For Milestone 77 we ran a fully automated combat simulator across 24 scenarios (tank, elite, group, and boss encounters in each of Acts 1–6) and replayed each one 200 times per subject, producing roughly — total combat resolutions for this snapshot.

Every hero class was tested at levels 1, 5, 10, and 15 with its starting kit. Companions, tap weapons, and tap utilities were each rolled into the same scenario harness so that DPS, HPS, and shield numbers are directly comparable across categories. Damage and healing figures are per round, averaged across all 24 scenarios.

The methodology deliberately fixes hero gear at the level-appropriate baseline (no luck of the drop) so the resulting numbers describe the kit, not a particular run. A drift chart at the bottom of this page will be populated as we capture the next milestone snapshots.

      Methodology notes

      • Scenarios: tank / elite / group / boss × Acts 1–6, each with hand-tuned enemy stat blocks pulled from the live game data files.

      • Runs: 200 deterministic replays per subject per scenario, with seeded RNG so a re-run of the same snapshot reproduces identical numbers.

      • Subjects: 19 hero classes, 10 companions, 10 tap weapons, 10 tap utilities — each tested independently against the same scenario suite.

      • Live tool: the same harness is exposed interactively at rebalance.html, where you can re-run a single subject, swap stat overrides, or capture a fresh snapshot for the next milestone.
