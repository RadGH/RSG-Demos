## Sets and Uniques System

Milestone 305 is the largest single item-system overhaul to date, adding two full tiers of named equipment above rare.

### Set Items (24 Sets)

Sets are organized in three tiers:

- **12 two-piece sets** — Sigon-style starter sets with mild but reliable bonuses. Designed for early Act 1-2 progression without demanding a full party commit.
- **8 three-piece mid-tier sets** — Require some build coordination. Bonuses per piece are modest; the third-piece completion bonus is strong.
- **4 five-piece endgame sets** — Full-slot commitments tuned for Act 5-6. Each set has a unique visual tint on equipped characters in the party panel.

Set bonus UI is surfaced in the inventory screen — orange text on partial completion, gold on full.

### Unique Items (24 Uniques)

24 named items distributed across acts 1 through 5, each with a mechanic not achievable via normal affixes:

- **Act 1-2 uniques** — Straightforward power outliers; best-in-slot for one stat at low levels.
- **Act 3 uniques** — Begin introducing conditional effects (on-hit, on-low-HP).
- **Act 4-5 uniques** — Full mechanical hooks: onCrit, onKill, onCast, onCombatStart. Legendary-quality effect power.

### 40 New Affixes

Affix coverage expanded to armor secondary slots, off-hand weapons, and jewelry. Notable additions include resistances split by damage type (fire, shadow, physical, arcane) and a new Tap Power affix tier above the existing range.

### Boss Loot Tables

Boss encounters in Act 3+ now have unique-drop entries baked into their loot tables. Each boss has at least one thematic unique that has a 5-10% drop chance on first kill.
