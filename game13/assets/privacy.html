Privacy Policy

What Emberveil collects, why, and how to opt out.

Anonymous Gameplay Telemetry (opt-in)

If you opt in, Emberveil sends anonymous gameplay events so we can balance classes, identify overpowered spells, and tune difficulty to real player experience. Opt-in is off by default. You are asked once on first launch and can flip the toggle anytime in Settings.

What we collect when you opt in

  • Random session ID (a UUID generated on your device, stored in browser localStorage)
  • Game version (e.g. M211)
  • Combat encounter results: act number, encounter ID, outcome, rounds elapsed, party size
  • Level-up events: class, level, attribute allocation
  • Per-combat damage aggregates: class, weapon category, average and max damage dealt
  • Tap weapon usage: weapon ID, hit counts, average damage
  • Run summaries: act reached, encounters cleared, outcome

What we do NOT collect

  • No character names, hero names, or any text you enter
  • No email address, account ID, or login identifiers
  • No IP address logged server-side beyond what is required for the request to reach our backend
  • No device fingerprinting, cookies for tracking, or cross-site identifiers
  • No screenshots, save files, or chat content

How the data is used

Only to tune game balance. Aggregated results may be published in milestone reports at /assets/. Raw events are never shared with third parties and are retained only as long as needed for the current balance patch analysis.

How to opt out

  • Open Settings in-game
  • Toggle Share Anonymous Data off
  • No further events will be sent. Previously queued events on your device are discarded the next time the game loads.

Cloud account (separate feature)

If you use the optional Cloud Saves feature, email + password are stored by our auth provider solely to authenticate your save file syncs. That system is independent from telemetry and is governed by our auth provider's own privacy policy.
