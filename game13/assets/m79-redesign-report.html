SFX Loudness Normalization

Every sound effect was run through ffmpeg loudnorm targeting EBU R128 -16 LUFS with -1.5 dB true peak ceiling. The chain lightning was no longer eardrum-punishing and the weapon swings were no longer whispers.

Before = preserved in public/sfx_original/, After = live public/sfx/. Play both and listen for the volume consistency.

Combat facing rework

Party members now face east (toward the enemy line) in combat instead of south (toward the camera). When a party member performs a melee attack, the sprite briefly swaps to its east_attack variant; spell casts swap to east_spell; incapacitated members swap to death. Transitions revert to idle after ~300–400ms via the existing stance state machine.

HP bars were also raised by ~18px so they no longer overlap the sprite's head — a long-standing visual bug from the M64 build.
