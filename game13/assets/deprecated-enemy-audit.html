Enemy Audit

How to use: Review every enemy in game13 and mark whether their current stats feel balanced, overtuned, or undertuned. Edit any field that needs to change, add notes, and optionally describe a visual rework. When finished, click Generate Prompt to produce a paste-back instruction Claude will use to apply the changes via src/maps/mapData.js.

Status legend: BALANCED OVERTUNED UNDERTUNED UNSET

Note: Enemy stats are currently authored raw in mapData.js (not formula-derived). Once the formulas.js refactor lands, role+tier curves will compute most of these. This survey is the input that drives the curve calibration.
