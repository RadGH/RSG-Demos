Edits applied (M369 — Hard Mode)

  • All regular enemies: HP × 4.0, damage × 2.0 (54 enemy stat blocks across ENEMIES, ENEMIES_ACT4, ENEMIES_ACT5, ENEMIES_ACT6, and inline encounter entries).
  • All boss enemies (19 bosses incl. hidden): HP × 7.0, damage × 2.0. Bosses identified by id (goblin_warlord, grax_veil_touched, veilspawn_herald, lava_titan, archfiend_malgrath, emberveil_sovereign, the_unraveler, the_architect, ancient_dragon, dragon_king, vault_guardian, void_scholar, echo_sovereign, the_first_ember) plus the "Veil High Priest" inline override.
  • Hero stats, items, skills, XP curve unchanged (per spec).
  • No difficulty toggle — buff applies unconditionally. The user wants to feel the floor.
  • Result: solo Knight (Comp C) survival 0% across Acts 1–6; Comp A/B 0% from Act 2 onward, ~10% Act 1.

Edits applied (M350)

  • Act 5 enemies (ENEMIES_ACT5): HP −27% to −29%, dmg −15% to −17%. Comp A win rate 32.1% → 77.5%; comp B 0.6% → 53.4%.
  • Act 6 dragons: HP +30% to +50% across whelp/ancient/king + regular dragon dmg slight bump. Comp A 88.4% → 85.8%; comp B 84.9% → 66.6%.
  • Act 3/4 untouched — already inside acceptable band.
  • Hero stats, items, skills, XP curve unchanged.
