Infinite Depths
Emberveil M306 — Post-game infinite scaling dungeon
Overview

What is the Infinite Depths?

A separate, post-game challenge mode accessible from the town screen after defeating at least one Act 5 boss (Emberveil Sovereign or The Unraveler). Each floor is a small procedurally generated dungeon with 3-5 nodes: combat encounters, optional treasure caches, and anchor bosses every 5 floors. There is no story, no fixed map — only the question of how deep you can go.

Rules

Death ends the run. All loot collected this run stays in your inventory. You may voluntarily exit at the end of any floor. Hardcore death triggers the M298 fallen-hero monument as normal. Best floors are tracked on the local leaderboard per character class.

Scaling Formula
Enemy HP = base_hp × 1.15floor (capped at 1,000,000)
Enemy Damage= base_dmg × 1.10floor
Enemy Level = max(party_avg_level, 5 + floor)
XP reward = base_xp × 1.08floor
Gold reward = base_gold × 1.06floor

Anchor bosses receive an additional +25% HP and +15% damage on top of the above scaling.

Floor Effects

Each floor rolls 1 effect (floor 1-4) or 1-2 effects (floor 5+). Effects are seeded by floor number and run seed — they are stable on re-entry.

Anchor Bosses Every 5 Floors

Every 5th floor is an Anchor floor. The final node is a scaled boss drawn from the pool below. Defeating an anchor boss guarantees a legendary-quality item drop.
