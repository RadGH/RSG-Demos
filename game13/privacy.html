Privacy & Telemetry

Last updated 2026-04-28

Emberveil is a single-developer hobby project. We collect the minimum information needed to operate cloud saves, understand what's broken, and improve the game. Everything optional is gated on the consent banner — declining keeps you in pure local-only mode.

Short version: nothing leaves your browser unless you click Accept on the consent banner. Local saves work either way; cloud saves, telemetry, and Google Analytics only run with your consent.

1. The consent banner

On first visit, a banner appears asking whether to enable analytics + telemetry. Your choice is stored in localStorage.rsg_consent_v1 on your device, and is honored across this domain. The banner only appears once; it reappears if you clear browser data or click Reset consent below.

The same key is read by both staging (radgh.github.io/RSG-Demos/game13/) and the live build at emberveil.radgh.com, but only within each domain — GDPR consent is per-controller, so a separate banner appears on each origin.

2. Google Analytics 4

If consent is granted, the page loads gtag.js from Google and reports anonymous page-view + interaction events to one of two GA4 properties depending on host:

  • G-XP7B7KVNY8 — staging (radgh.github.io)
  • G-5H26ZXKBCL — production (emberveil.radgh.com)

IP anonymization is on by default (anonymize_ip: true). If consent is not granted, gtag.js is never loaded — no requests to Google's servers are made and no cookies are set.

3. Internal telemetry (Supabase)

If consent is granted and you are signed in to a Supabase account (optional), the game records a small set of gameplay events to telemetry_events: combat outcomes, screen visits, reward acceptance, and run-end stats. We use this to spot bugs and tune balance — never to identify users.

Without consent the telemetry client silently no-ops. Without sign-in, no row is ever written. You can review the schema at public/docs/supabase-setup.md.

4. Cloud saves & sign-in

Cloud saves are a feature for signed-in users. They store an encrypted snapshot of your party, inventory, and run state in Supabase under your account. Local saves are not cloud-synced unless you explicitly use Save to cloud. Sign-in is via Supabase auth and uses the email you provide; we do not see your password.

  • Local saves: always work. Stored in localStorage. Never leave your browser.
  • Cloud saves: opt-in per save. Encrypted before upload. Removable via account deletion.

5. What we don't collect

  • No precise location.
  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No keystroke or input recording.
  • No third-party trackers besides GA4 (and only with consent).
  • No cross-site fingerprinting.

7. Children & age

Emberveil is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe we have, contact us via the contact page and we will delete it.
